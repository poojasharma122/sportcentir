Personal Data Policy

Sport Centir (“we,” “our,” “us”) is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Personal Data Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard the personal information you share with us, and reaffirms our commitment to never sell or rent your data to third parties.

1. What We Mean by Personal Data

For the purposes of this policy, “personal data” means any information that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly. This may include your name, email address, IP address, and any other details you provide through our website, sportcentir.com.

2. How We Collect Personal Data

We collect personal data when

  • You contact us via email or through our contact form.

  • You subscribe to our newsletter.

  • You participate in surveys, feedback forms, or other voluntary submissions.

  • You browse our website, through cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookies Policy).

3. How We Use Personal Data

We only use personal data for purposes that you have agreed to or that are necessary for operating our site, including:

  • Responding to your inquiries.

  • Sending newsletters or updates you have opted in to receive.

  • Analysing site usage to improve content and services.

  • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Our Commitment to Privacy

  • We never sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

  • We only share data with trusted service providers necessary to operate our site, and only under strict confidentiality agreements.

  • We collect the minimum data necessary for each interaction.

5. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction. Data is stored securely and only accessible to authorised personnel. While we take security seriously, no online transmission or storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.

  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.

  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.

  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@sportcentir.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Personal Data Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated “Last Updated” date. Continued use of our site after updates indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
