NFL Fantasy Football 2025: Draft Strategies for a Winning Season

With the NFL season approaching, now’s the time to sharpen your draft game. Start by targeting high-volume running backs early — they remain the most reliable weekly scorers. Keep an eye on players in contract years, as they often deliver career performances. If you’re picking late in the first round, doubling up on elite wide receivers can give you a consistent points advantage. Don’t forget about bye weeks; stacking too many key players off in the same week can derail momentum. Finally, resist the urge to grab a quarterback too early — the depth at the position means value can be found later in the draft.
