NHL Fantasy Hockey 2025: Building a Championship Roster

A winning fantasy hockey roster in 2025 will balance proven stars with breakout candidates. Secure at least one elite goaltender early, as reliable netminders are in short supply. Prioritise multi-position forwards — they give you flexibility to cover injuries and maximise games played. Keep an eye on power-play units in pre-season; players in prime special teams roles often outperform their draft position. Late in the draft, target high-upside rookies from strong offensive teams. And don’t be afraid to make early-season waiver moves — aggressive management in October often pays off in April.
