Avoiding Common Fantasy Mistakes: Lessons from Past Seasons

Every fantasy manager has made a decision they regret — drafting with bias, chasing one-week wonders, or ignoring bye weeks. The most common mistakes include overvaluing players from your favourite team, ignoring the waiver wire until it’s too late, and making trades based purely on name recognition. Successful managers stick to their strategy, stay active throughout the season, and separate emotion from decision-making. Learn from past errors and approach each season with a fresh mindset.
