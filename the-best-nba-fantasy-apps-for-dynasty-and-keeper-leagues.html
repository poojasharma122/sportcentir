The Best NBA Fantasy Apps for Dynasty and Keeper Leagues

Season-long leagues are fun, but dynasty and keeper formats let you manage your roster for years. Choosing the right platform can make a huge difference.

Fantrax: Deep player pools, flexible keeper rules, and support for multi-year contracts.

Sleeper: Sleek design with custom scoring and trade flexibility, ideal for active long-term leagues.

MyFantasyLeague (basketball version): Rarely used for NBA but offers advanced tools for dedicated commissioners.

Verdict: Fantrax remains the top choice for serious dynasty managers, with Sleeper a close second for its modern interface and engagement features.
